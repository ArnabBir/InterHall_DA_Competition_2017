Stats and Demographics for the 95680 ZIP Code

ZIP code 95680 is located in west California and covers a slightly less than average land area compared to other ZIP codes in the United States. It also has a slightly less than average population density.

The people living in ZIP code 95680 are primarily white. The number of people in their late 20s to early 40s is extremely large while the number of middle aged adults is slightly higher than average. There are also a large number of families and a small number of single adults. The percentage of children under 18 living in the 95680 ZIP code is extremely large compared to other areas of the country.

Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work

As with most parts of the country, vehicles are the most common form of transportation to places of employment. Pedestrians and cyclists beware. In most parts of the country, the majority of commuters get to work in under half an hour. However, that is not the case in 95680. It has a much lower than average number of people that make it to work in under half an hour.
