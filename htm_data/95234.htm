Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work

As with most parts of the country, vehicles are the most common form of transportation to places of employment. If you are a person that likes walking or biking to work, it will be a comfort for you to know that 95234 has a well above average number of people who do not use a vehicle to commute. A higher percentage of people in 95234 than almost anywhere in the country avoid a commute altogether by working at home. In most parts of the country, the majority of commuters get to work in under half an hour. However, that is not the case in 95234. It has a much lower than average number of people that make it to work in under half an hour.
