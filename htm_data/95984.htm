Stats and Demographics for the 95984 ZIP Code

ZIP code 95984 is located in northwest California and covers a slightly less than average land area compared to other ZIP codes in the United States. It also has a slightly less than average population density.

The people living in ZIP code 95984 are primarily white. The number of middle aged adults is extremely large while the number of people in their late 20s to early 40s is extremely small. There are also a large number of single adults and a small number of single parents. The percentage of children under 18 living in the 95984 ZIP code is extremely small compared to other areas of the country.
