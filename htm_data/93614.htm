Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work

The median household income of $46,551 is compared to the rest of the country. It is also compared to nearby ZIP codes. While money isn't everything, ZIP code 93614 may not be as nice as other parts of town.

As with most parts of the country, vehicles are the most common form of transportation to places of employment. Instead of a commute, a higher percentage of people in 93614 are able to work from home than most places in the nation. In most parts of the country, the majority of commuters get to work in under half an hour. In 93614 though, the percentage of people that make it to work in under half an hour is among the lowest. It is much more common for employees to have to travel over 45 minutes to their place of employment than most parts of country which could indicate bad traffic or that people typically live far from where they work.
