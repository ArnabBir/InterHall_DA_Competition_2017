Real Estate and Housing

ZIP code 92313 has a small percentage of vacancies. The Census also indicates that there are one or more nursing homes nearby.

The majority of household are owned or have a mortgage. Homes in ZIP code 92313 were primarily built in the 1970s or the 1980s. Looking at 92313 real estate data, the median home value of $232,300 is slightly higher than average compared to the rest of the country. Rentals in 92313 are most commonly 2 bedrooms. The rent for 2 bedrooms is normally $1,000+/month including utilities. 1 bedrooms are also common and rent for $750-$999/month. Prices for rental property include ZIP code 92313 apartments, townhouses, and homes that are primary residences.
