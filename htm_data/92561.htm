Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work

The median household income of $82,344 is compared to the rest of the country. It is also compared to nearby ZIP codes. So 92561 is likely to be one of the nicer parts of town with a more affluent demographic.

As with most parts of the country, vehicles are the most common form of transportation to places of employment. Pedestrians and cyclists beware. The area has some of lowest percentages of commutes without a vehicle in the country. A higher percentage of people in 92561 than almost anywhere in the country avoid a commute altogether by working at home.
