Real Estate and Housing

The majority of household are owned or have a mortgage. Homes in ZIP code 90041 were primarily built in 1939 or earlier. Looking at 90041 real estate data, the median home value of $535,500 is extremely high compared to the rest of the country. It is also slightly less than average compared to nearby ZIP codes. 90041 could be an area to look for cheap housing compared to surrounding areas. Rentals in 90041 are most commonly 2 bedrooms.
