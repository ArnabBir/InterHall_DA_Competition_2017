Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work

The median household income of $65,223 is compared to the rest of the country. It is also compared to nearby ZIP codes. While money isn't everything, ZIP code 95452 may not be as nice as other parts of town.

As with most parts of the country, vehicles are the most common form of transportation to places of employment. ZIP code 95452 uses public transportation to get to work more than almost anywhere in the country. Pedestrians and cyclists beware. The area has some of lowest percentages of commutes without a vehicle in the country. In most parts of the country, the majority of commuters get to work in under half an hour.
