Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work

The median household income of $42,338 is compared to the rest of the country. It is also compared to nearby ZIP codes. So 92252 is likely to be one of the nicer parts of town with a more affluent demographic.

As with most parts of the country, vehicles are the most common form of transportation to places of employment. Instead of a commute, a higher percentage of people in 92252 are able to work from home than most places in the nation. In most parts of the country, the majority of commuters get to work in under half an hour. However, that is not the case in 92252. It has a much lower than average number of people that make it to work in under half an hour.
