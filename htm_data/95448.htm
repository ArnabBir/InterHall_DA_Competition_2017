Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work

As with most parts of the country, vehicles are the most common form of transportation to places of employment. If you are a person that likes walking or biking to work, it will be a comfort for you to know that 95448 has a well above average number of people who do not use a vehicle to commute. Instead of a commute, a higher percentage of people in 95448 are able to work from home than most places in the nation. In most parts of the country, the majority of commuters get to work in under half an hour. More commuters in 95448 get to experience these short commute times than most other ZIP codes.
