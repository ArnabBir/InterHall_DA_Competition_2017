Stats and Demographics for the 95325 ZIP Code

ZIP code 95325 is located in central California and covers a slightly less than average land area compared to other ZIP codes in the United States. It also has a slightly less than average population density.

The people living in ZIP code 95325 are primarily white. The number of seniors is extremely large while the number of people in their late 20s to early 40s is large. There are also a large number of families and a slightly less than average number of single parents. The percentage of children under 18 living in the 95325 ZIP code is slightly less than average compared to other areas of the country.

Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work

As with most parts of the country, vehicles are the most common form of transportation to places of employment. If you are a person that likes walking or biking to work, it will be a comfort for you to know that 95325 has a well above average number of people who do not use a vehicle to commute. Compared to other ZIP codes in the country, 95325 has very few people that work at home. In most parts of the country, the majority of commuters get to work in under half an hour. In 95325 though, the percentage of people that make it to work in under half an hour is among the lowest. The area has an extremely high percentage of people that most travel over 45 minutes to their place of employment. People that commute should be aware of the potentially long commute times.
