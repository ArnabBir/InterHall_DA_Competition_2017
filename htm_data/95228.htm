Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work

The median household income of $61,488 is compared to the rest of the country. It is also compared to nearby ZIP codes. So 95228 is likely to be one of the nicer parts of town with a more affluent demographic.

As with most parts of the country, vehicles are the most common form of transportation to places of employment. Pedestrians and cyclists beware.
