Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work

The median household income of $99,716 is compared to the rest of the country. It is also compared to nearby ZIP codes. While money isn't everything, ZIP code 94020 may not be as nice as other parts of town.

As with most parts of the country, vehicles are the most common form of transportation to places of employment. A higher percentage of people in 94020 than almost anywhere in the country avoid a commute altogether by working at home. In most parts of the country, the majority of commuters get to work in under half an hour. In 94020 though, the percentage of people that make it to work in under half an hour is among the lowest.
