Real Estate and Housing

Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work

The median household income of $23,750 is compared to the rest of the country. It is also compared to nearby ZIP codes. While money isn't everything, ZIP code 96110 may not be as nice as other parts of town.

ZIP 96110 is unusual in that "Bicycle, Walked, or Other Means" is the most frequent method of transportation to work instead of vehicles. 96110 is extremely friendly to pedestrians/cyclists and the percentage of people using these transportation methods to commute to work are among the highest in the country. A higher percentage of people in 96110 than almost anywhere in the country avoid a commute altogether by working at home. In most parts of the country, the majority of commuters get to work in under half an hour. A slightly higher than average number of commuters in 96110 can expect to fall in that range. It is very uncommon, compared to the rest of the US, for employees to have to travel more than 45 minutes to their place of employment.
