Stats and Demographics for the 95033 ZIP Code

ZIP code 95033 is located in west California and covers a slightly higher than average land area compared to other ZIP codes in the United States. It also has a slightly less than average population density.

Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work

The median household income of $116,382 is compared to the rest of the country. It is also compared to nearby ZIP codes. So 95033 is likely to be one of the nicer parts of town with a more affluent demographic.

As with most parts of the country, vehicles are the most common form of transportation to places of employment. Instead of a commute, a higher percentage of people in 95033 are able to work from home than most places in the nation. In most parts of the country, the majority of commuters get to work in under half an hour. In 95033 though, the percentage of people that make it to work in under half an hour is among the lowest. It is much more common for employees to have to travel over 45 minutes to their place of employment than most parts of country which could indicate bad traffic or that people typically live far from where they work.
