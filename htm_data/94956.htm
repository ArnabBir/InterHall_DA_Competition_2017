Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work

The median household income of $57,105 is compared to the rest of the country. It is also compared to nearby ZIP codes. While money isn't everything, ZIP code 94956 may not be as nice as other parts of town.

As with most parts of the country, vehicles are the most common form of transportation to places of employment. 94956 is extremely friendly to pedestrians/cyclists and the percentage of people using these transportation methods to commute to work are among the highest in the country. A higher percentage of people in 94956 than almost anywhere in the country avoid a commute altogether by working at home. In most parts of the country, the majority of commuters get to work in under half an hour. However, that is not the case in 94956. It has a much lower than average number of people that make it to work in under half an hour.
