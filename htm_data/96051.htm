Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work

The median household income of $46,304 is compared to the rest of the country. It is also compared to nearby ZIP codes. So 96051 is likely to be one of the nicer parts of town with a more affluent demographic.

As with most parts of the country, vehicles are the most common form of transportation to places of employment. Pedestrians and cyclists beware. The area has some of lowest percentages of commutes without a vehicle in the country. Instead of a commute, a higher percentage of people in 96051 are able to work from home than most places in the nation. In most parts of the country, the majority of commuters get to work in under half an hour. In 96051 though, the percentage of people that make it to work in under half an hour is among the lowest. It is very uncommon, compared to the rest of the US, for employees to have to travel more than 45 minutes to their place of employment.
