Real Estate and Housing

ZIP code 93510 has a small percentage of vacancies. The Census also indicates that there are one or more correctional facilities nearby.

Looking at 93510 real estate data, the median home value of $427,300 is extremely high compared to the rest of the country. It is also high compared to nearby ZIP codes. So you are less likely to find inexpensive homes in 93510. Rentals in 93510 are most commonly 3+ bedrooms. The rent for 3+ bedrooms is normally $1,000+/month including utilities. Prices for rental property include ZIP code 93510 apartments, townhouses, and homes that are primary residences.

Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work
