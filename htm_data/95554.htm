Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work

The median household income of $31,107 is compared to the rest of the country. It is also compared to nearby ZIP codes. So 95554 is likely to be one of the nicer parts of town with a more affluent demographic.

The area has some of lowest percentages of commutes without a vehicle in the country. Compared to other ZIP codes in the country, 95554 has very few people that work at home. In most parts of the country, the majority of commuters get to work in under half an hour. In 95554 though, the percentage of people that make it to work in under half an hour is among the lowest.
