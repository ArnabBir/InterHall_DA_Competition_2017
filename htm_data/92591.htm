Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work

The median household income of $68,275 is compared to the rest of the country. It is also compared to nearby ZIP codes. While money isn't everything, ZIP code 92591 may not be as nice as other parts of town.

As with most parts of the country, vehicles are the most common form of transportation to places of employment. In most parts of the country, the majority of commuters get to work in under half an hour. However, that is not the case in 92591. It has a much lower than average number of people that make it to work in under half an hour.
