Real Estate and Housing

ZIP code 91730 has a small percentage of vacancies. The Census also indicates that there are one or more correctional facilities nearby.

The majority of household are rented. Homes in ZIP code 91730 were primarily built in the 2000s or the 1980s. Looking at 91730 real estate data, the median home value of $283,700 is high compared to the rest of the country. It is also slightly less than average compared to nearby ZIP codes. 91730 could be an area to look for cheap housing compared to surrounding areas. Rentals in 91730 are most commonly 2 bedrooms. The rent for 2 bedrooms is normally $1,000+/month including utilities. 1 bedrooms are also common and rent for $1,000+/month. Prices for rental property include ZIP code 91730 apartments, townhouses, and homes that are primary residences.
