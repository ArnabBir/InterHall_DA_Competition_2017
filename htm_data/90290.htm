Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work

The median household income of $118,750 is compared to the rest of the country. It is also compared to nearby ZIP codes. So 90290 is likely to be one of the nicer parts of town with a more affluent demographic.

As with most parts of the country, vehicles are the most common form of transportation to places of employment. A higher percentage of people in 90290 than almost anywhere in the country avoid a commute altogether by working at home. In most parts of the country, the majority of commuters get to work in under half an hour. In 90290 though, the percentage of people that make it to work in under half an hour is among the lowest.
