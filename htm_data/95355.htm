Real Estate and Housing

ZIP code 95355 has a small percentage of vacancies. The Census also indicates that there are one or more correctional facilities and nursing homes nearby.

Looking at 95355 real estate data, the median home value of $195,400 is slightly higher than average compared to the rest of the country. It is also high compared to nearby ZIP codes. So you are less likely to find inexpensive homes in 95355. Rentals in 95355 are most commonly 2 bedrooms.
