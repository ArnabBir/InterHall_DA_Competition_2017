Stats and Demographics for the 95135 ZIP Code

Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work

The median household income of $124,288 is compared to the rest of the country. It is also compared to nearby ZIP codes. So 95135 is likely to be one of the nicer parts of town with a more affluent demographic.

As with most parts of the country, vehicles are the most common form of transportation to places of employment. In most parts of the country, the majority of commuters get to work in under half an hour. In 95135 though, the percentage of people that make it to work in under half an hour is among the lowest.
