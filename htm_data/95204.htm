Real Estate and Housing

ZIP code 95204 has a small percentage of vacancies. The Census also indicates that there are one or more nursing homes and universities nearby.

The majority of household are owned or have a mortgage. Homes in ZIP code 95204 were primarily built in the 1950s or the 1940s. Looking at 95204 real estate data, the median home value of $140,400 is slightly less than average compared to the rest of the country.
