Employment, Income, Earnings, and Work

The median household income of $87,708 is compared to the rest of the country. It is also compared to nearby ZIP codes. While money isn't everything, ZIP code 94950 may not be as nice as other parts of town.

As with most parts of the country, vehicles are the most common form of transportation to places of employment. 94950 is extremely friendly to pedestrians/cyclists and the percentage of people using these transportation methods to commute to work are among the highest in the country. Compared to other ZIP codes in the country, 94950 has very few people that work at home. In most parts of the country, the majority of commuters get to work in under half an hour.
